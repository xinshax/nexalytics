  1. Direct-to-consumer (DTC) model: The brand sells its skincare products directly to consumers through its website and social media channels, which helps to reduce retail markups and offer affordable pricing to customers.
  2. Ingredient transparency: The brand prioritizes ingredient transparency and provides detailed information about the ingredients used in its products, which helps to build trust with customers and establish the brand as a reliable source of skincare products.
  3. Personalized skincare solutions: The brand offers customized skincare solutions based on an individual’s skin type and concerns, which helps to create a loyal customer base and drive repeat purchases.
  4. Influencer marketing: The brand collaborates with beauty bloggers, influencers, and skincare experts to promote its products on social media platforms like Instagram, which helps to build brand awareness and reach a wider audience.
  5. Social media engagement: The brand actively engages with its customers on social media platforms, responding to queries and comments, sharing user-generated content, and offering skincare advice. This helps to build a sense of community and loyalty among its customers.

Overall, the Minimalist brand’s DTC model, ingredient transparency, personalized skincare solutions, influencer marketing, and social media engagement have all contributed to its success. These strategies have helped to establish the brand as a trustworthy and reliable source of effective and affordable skincare products.

What makes them different?

Brand Presentation : As the name says Everything about this brand is Minimal. In Indian market where all skin care brands were splashing colour full packaging with random fruits and ayurvedic herbs photo on packaging, Minimalist was like f em, we are gonna be different. And so when they started their production they made the packaging so simple that they only used black and white colour and only wrote what all chemicals are used in the product, No fruits no colour or nothing of that sort. This made them Stand out from the mass of skin care brands. Thats how they started getting noticed in Indian market.

Business Strategies??

Minimalist had a lot of business strategies which helped them to be a successful skincare brand. But what Minimalist focused the most was Social Media Marketing. They knew their potential customers, the beauty conscious teens and people where scrolling through Instagram and they were ready to use it to reach each and everyone of em.

The brand has an active presence on social media platforms like Instagram, where it regularly shares user-generated content, skincare tips, and product information. The brand also responds promptly to comments and messages from customers, which helps to build a sense of trust and connection.

One specific example of this strategy is the Minimalist’s “Ask The Expert” series on Instagram. In this series, the brand invites skincare experts to answer questions from customers about specific skin concerns. The experts provide detailed and personalized advice, which helps to build trust with customers and establishes the brand as a reliable source of skincare information.

For example, in a recent “Ask The Expert” session, the Minimalist invited a dermatologist to answer questions about how to care for sensitive skin. The dermatologist provided detailed advice about the best ingredients and products for sensitive skin, and also recommended specific Minimalist products that would be suitable for this skin type. Customers were able to ask follow-up questions and receive personalized advice, which helped to build a sense of community and loyalty.

By using social media engagement to build a community of loyal customers, the Minimalist has been able to establish itself as a trusted and reliable source of skincare products and information. This strategy has helped to differentiate the brand from competitors and drive sales growth.
